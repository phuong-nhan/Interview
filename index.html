Jungim and Graphic Design

INTERVIEW BY LUCETTE

1. Why do you want to be a designer?

I want to be a designer because since I was young I really liked art and drawing I just love expressing what I think And I think that designer is the best occupation.

2. What makes you nervous about becoming a designer?

I feel nervous always due to I am not sure that my designs are enough to satisfy consumers and clients.

3. What excites you about design?

Making my ideas and sketches to real is making me feel exciting.

4. What are your strengths?

Illustration, simplifying. I can make information as simple.

5. What are some areas of opportunity to work on?

Illustrator, photographer, web designer.

6. What aspects of graphic design to you gravitate towards?

Basically, graphic designer can handle a various tools. I love learn new and various things. So that is the one thing gravitating me into graphic design.

7. What are the roles and responsibilities of a graphic design in society?

Making information simple and convey them with accurate meaning to consumers.

8. When/where/why are you at your most creative/productive?

I think college is the place that I can focus on my work well. Because when I work at college I feel I should do something. Actually, I can be creative when I am not at home.

9. What are your comfort levels with giving and receiving feedback? How might you improve your skills here? What strategies have worked for you?

If there is maximum 10, I can give feedback with 4~5 level and I can receive feedback with 8~9 level. Because I think receiving feedback is really important to my overall works. Leaning basic things thoroughly and trying everything even those are not making sense. Trying and failing is really important experiences. Learning from experienced people and getting feedbacks. Trying every single things.

10. What is a website?

I can say website is one of the ways to convey culture, opinion, arguments, and information to people.
